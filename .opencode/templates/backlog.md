**Legend:**
- `[ ]` = TODO (storia creata, non ancora pianificata)
- `[P]` = PLANNED (task definiti, pronta per sviluppo)
- `[~]` = IN PROGRESS (sviluppo in corso)
- `[x]` = DONE (completata)
- `[!]` = BLOCKED (bloccata)

**Priority Levels:**
- **HIGH** - Critical, must-have for MVP
- **MEDIUM** - Important, should-have
- **LOW** - Nice-to-have, can-wait

**Story Points:**
- 1-2pt: Small (< 1 day)
- 3-5pt: Medium (1-3 days)
- 8pt: Large (< 1 week, consider splitting)
